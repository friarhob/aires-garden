!!! note "About this story"
    There was an internet competition called Business Card Fiction (sometime in the late 2000s / early 2010s). The idea was to write a short story that could fit on the back of a business card. I submitted this once, and although it has some serious English grammar errors, it got an honourable mention by the judges.

![A fantasy/medieval like open theatre viewed from above. Under that the short story that says: Cry -- Everybody went, from all parts of the city, to the festival. There was a rumor about a singer who could, through her song, take away the sadness from every heart. And everybody saw that blonde little girl, with long braids falling over a rustic wooden guitar, and they heard her to sing her song. It was a gentle voice, with gentle sounds coming from the struggle between her tiny fingers and the strong strings. A few minutes later, everybody was happy again.
